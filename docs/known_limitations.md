# Known Limitations

- FoilBrief is decision support, not automated boat control.
- Maneuver labels are rule-based candidate labels, not ground-truth human labels.
- Fleet median is a comparison baseline, not a reference path for best performance.
- Signed VMG has polarity caveats; the final demo uses speed-first and absolute-VMG evidence.
- The Time Loss Receipt is an estimated review signal, not measured elapsed-time attribution.
- Recovery status is limited to the +30 second review window used in the demo pack.
- The primary case is a single validated demo case, not a claim about every race condition.
- The coach or analyst makes the final decision.
